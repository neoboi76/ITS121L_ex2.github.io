Living Room


This luxurious living room is a sanctuary of style and comfort, boasting exquisite details and thoughtful design elements that elevate the space to unparalleled levels of sophistication. The built-in cabinets offer ample storage while seamlessly blending into the room's aesthetic, adding both functionality and elegance. The sleek tile flooring not only lends a touch of modernity but also ensures easy maintenance, making it ideal for high-traffic areas. The pièce de résistance of the room undoubtedly lies in its breathtaking views of the White Tank Mountains, framed by two expansive picture windows and a sliding glass door that flood the space with natural light while inviting the serene beauty of the outdoors inside. Nestled amidst this picturesque backdrop, the fireplace takes center stage, its art deco mantel adding a dash of timeless charm and refinement to the 20' x 14' room. Whether it's a quiet evening spent curled up with loved ones or a lively gathering of friends, this versatile space effortlessly accommodates every occasion, promising unforgettable moments against the backdrop of majestic sunsets and mountain vistas.
